Red&White

Not Just A Test

Although serving as a little taste test for what was to come next year for his album, Pink Tape, this EP not only showcases Lil Uzi Vert's versatility in the studio but his ability to complete a compelling body of work at the same time. This EP contains a mix of different styles and sounds that highlight his growth as an artist, with songs like F.F., GLOCK IN MY PURSE, and a what is now know as a classic in his catalouge, ISSA HIT. Most of the songs on this EP are upbeat, some with repetative but catchy hooks, similar to Playboi Carti's, Whole Lotta Red. Some songs unfortunatley fall flat such as the first two SPACE CADET and I KNOW, in which both songs drag and feel very slow compared to the others. The production of the album was definitley one of the standout features on this EP as every beat flowed well, were very versatile, and never made the vocals feel lost.
