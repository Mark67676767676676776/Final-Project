S1 EP 1:Ryomen Sukuna

A great start!

In this episode we are introduced to the main character, Yuji Itadori, a highschool student who is unexpectedly thrust into the world of curses after being attacked by a cursed spirit when he joins an occult club. In order to save a sorcerer on scene, Megumi Fushiguro, and his friends he consumes the finger of a legendary sorcerer, Ryomen Sukuna. This makes him become the host of the powerful curse and will change his life completely.
