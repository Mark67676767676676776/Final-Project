Snooze

No Snooze At All

Snooze is a song made by SZA, released in 2024 as the leading single of her album SOS. The song is a blend of R&B and pop, with a catchy beat and smooth vocals from SZA, written by Leon Thomas. The lyrics of the song revolve around SZA longing for a relationship with a romantic interest. The song has received positive reviews from critics and fans alike. Overall, Snooze is a standout track on the SOS album and showcases the talents of both SZA's singing ability and Leon Thomas's amazing writing.
